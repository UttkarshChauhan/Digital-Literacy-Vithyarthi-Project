## Project Overview
This Digital Literacy Portfolio was created as part of my role as a Student Digital Ambassador. The project focuses on developing essential skills required to navigate the digital world responsibly and effectively, including online identity, safe internet practices, professional communication, and cyber safety.

Through five structured tasks, I explored key aspects of digital literacy by combining practical work with reflection. This portfolio demonstrates my understanding of digital tools and responsible online behaviour.

---

## Tasks Summary

### Task 1: Digital Literacy Infographic
Created a one-page infographic using Canva explaining digital literacy, tools, safety, and professional presence.

### Task 2: Online Portfolio
Set up professional profiles on GitHub, LinkedIn, and Stack Overflow to establish a digital presence.

### Task 3: Platforms and Collaboration
Completed beginner-level Python challenges on HackerRank and created a Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz using Google Forms.  


### Task 4: Email Etiquette
Wrote two professional emails and created a checklist for responsible social media usage.

### Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness
Developed a case study on UPI fraud and created a prevention checklist for safe online practices.
